ZOSO

Led Zepplin

November 8, 1971

The untitled fourth studio album by English rock band Led Zeppelin, commonly known as Led Zeppelin IV, was released on 8 November 1971 by Atlantic Records.

Band Members

Robert Plant – lead and overdubbed backing vocals, tambourine, harmonica on "When the Levee Breaks"

Jimmy Page – electric and acoustic guitars, mandolin on "The Battle of Evermore",

John Paul Jones – bass guitar, electric piano, mandolin on "Going to California", recorders, EMS VCS 3, acoustic guitar on "The Battle of Evermore"

John Bonham – drums, percussion
